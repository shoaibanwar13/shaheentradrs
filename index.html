NARC G1 is an unparalleled variety of garlic. Its production has been recorded up to 250 million per acre this year. Which is the highest production of any variety of garlic in Pakistan. The most important advantage of this variety is its long shelf life. It does not spoil for a year if stored in a well-ventilated and shady place with stalks. If the farmer stores it well and sells it in winter, then the general market rate will also increase significantly..
